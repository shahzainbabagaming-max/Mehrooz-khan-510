1. Peacock
    • The peacock is known for its dazzling tail feathers, which fan out in a rainbow of shimmering blues and greens. It symbolizes beauty and elegance in many cultures, especially in India.
  2. White Tiger
    • The white tiger is a rare and stunning big cat with white fur and piercing blue eyes. It looks powerful and majestic, like a snow-covered king of the jungle.
  3. Blue Morpho Butterfly
    • This butterfly has brilliant blue wings that shine like jewels in the sunlight. Found in South America, it seems almost magical when it flutters through the rainforest.
  4. Arabian Horse
    • The Arabian horse has a slim body, a curved neck, and eyes full of expression. Its graceful movements and proud look make it one of the most beautiful horse breeds in the world.
  5. Snow Leopard
  6. The snow leopard lives high in the mountains and is rarely seen by humans. Its thick, spotted fur and long fluffy tail make it look both fierce and gentle at the same time.
