1. Portugal
    2. Portugal has a mild and sunny climate. Summers are warm and dry, while winters are cool but not too cold. The fresh ocean breeze makes the weather very comfortable.

  2. New Zealand
    3. New Zealand has a clean, green environment with mild temperatures. It doesn’t get too hot or too cold, especially on the North Island. The weather is great for outdoor activities all year.

  3. Greece
    4. Greece enjoys a Mediterranean climate with hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters. The sunny weather and blue skies attract tourists from around the world.

  4. Costa Rica
    5. Costa Rica has a tropical climate with warm temperatures all year. Some areas, like the Central Valley, feel like spring all the time. It’s perfect for nature lovers.

  5. Spain
    6. Spain has long, sunny summers and mild winters, especially in the south. Cities like Seville and Malaga get lots of sunshine. The dry air makes the heat feel comfortable.

  6. South Africa
    7. South Africa’s Cape Town has a Mediterranean-style climate. Summers are warm and dry, and winters are cool and rainy. The air is fresh, and the skies are often clear.
